    About Us

    This program offers you an opportunity to study at UINSA for one/two semesters and take a wide range of undergraduate or graduate program courses. To be able to participate in this program, you do not have to be registered as a student in one of UINSA partner universities.

    By joining the program, you can:
    1. study at UINSA for one or two semesters
    2. take a wide range of undergraduate or graduate courses
    3. obtain credit for study at UINSA towards your home university degree
    4. enhance your educational experience as well as career opportunities
    5. make new friends and take experience in learning diverse cultures

    Eligibility

    In general, you must:

    01

    have completed at least one year of full-time study at the time of application

    02

    have Bahasa Indonesia skill (if needed)

    03

    have achieved a minimum GPA of 3.00 or higher (on a 4-point scale, or its equivalent)

    04

    meet language requirements of the offered courses

    05

    be able to self-finance and pay for the tuition fee or during period of study at UINSA

    06

    have overseas health insurance
